About Staco

Redefining how developers build Single Page Applications. Simple, lightweight, and powerful.

The Mission

Staco was born from a simple idea: Web development shouldn't be complicated.

In a world of complex build tools, bundlers, and heavy frameworks, Staco offers a breath of fresh air. It proves that you can build modern, reactive, and scalable applications using just the web platform's native capabilities enhanced with lightweight libraries.
